This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled in mobile applications developed and published by piodev on Google Play.

> Applications Covered

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications by piodev:

  • Memory Trash Truck, Clean City (com.moewballs.memory.trash.truck.clean.the.city)
  • Kula Zgadula (com.moewballs.polski.quiz.znawcow.magii) — previously published as "Polski Quiz Znawców Magii"; same application, the package name has not changed
  • Color Match Car (com.moewballs.colormatchcar)
  • Tank 2000: Play Together (com.moewballs.tank2000playtogether)

Applications may be displayed under different localized titles depending on the user's language settings on Google Play. All localized versions refer to the same applications identified by their package names listed above.

> 1. Data We Collect

Our applications collect only data necessary for proper operation:

  • Technical data: Device model, operating system and version, application version, random device identifier (e.g. UUID)
  • App usage data: Game results, gameplay statistics, game progress
  • Profile data (stored locally only): Some applications let you create a local player profile (e.g. a nickname, an avatar, and optionally a date of birth used for entertainment features such as horoscope-style content). This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any server
  • Personal data: Not collected automatically. Personal data may only be provided voluntarily by the user (e.g. via email contact)

Applications do not collect personal data automatically, unless you consent to it.

> 2. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Ensuring proper operation of the application
  • Saving game progress
  • Improving gameplay and user experience
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues
  • Displaying ads and rewards (if the application uses ads)

The data is not used to identify individual users.

> 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Some of our applications may use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location based on IP address
  • App usage and ad interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

This data may be used by Google to personalize advertisements shown to the user. Not all applications use advertising. If an application does not currently display ads, this section does not apply to that application.

> 3a. Ad Personalization and Opt-Out

Advertisements displayed in our applications may be personalized based on your interests, app usage, and device information. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → disable "Personalized Ads"

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not reduce the number of ads shown; it only means the ads will be less relevant to your interests.

> 3b. Tracking Technologies

Our applications do not use cookies. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers and similar tracking technologies on your device to deliver and measure advertisements. These identifiers can be managed or reset through your device settings as described above.

> 3c. Rewarded Ads

Some of our applications offer optional rewarded ads: you may choose to watch an advertisement in exchange for a small in-game bonus (for example, a hint or a small amount of in-game currency). Watching rewarded ads is always voluntary and requires your explicit action — ads of this type are never shown without your consent. Rewards are virtual items usable only inside the application, have no monetary value, and are non-transferable.

> 3d. Ad Consent (EEA / UK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, before any ads are shown the application will display a consent dialog (Google's consent management platform) asking whether you agree to personalized advertising. You can decline and still use the application, and you can change your choice later in the application settings.

> 4. Data Sharing

User data is not sold and is not shared with third parties, with the exception of:

  • Advertising partners: If the application displays ads, advertising partners may collect technical device data for ad personalization
  • Legal authorities: If required by law (e.g. court orders)

> 5. Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, and alteration:

  • Encryption during data transmission (if applicable)
  • Access to data is limited to authorized persons only

> 6. Local Storage

Our applications may store game progress and settings locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server and remains only on your device.

> 6a. Camera and QR Codes

Some of our applications can use the device camera solely to scan QR codes (for example, to join a local multiplayer room created by another player). The camera image is processed on your device in real time only for code detection. No photos or videos are taken, stored, or transmitted anywhere. The camera permission is optional — if you deny it, the application remains fully usable and the same action can be completed without scanning (e.g. by selecting a room from a list).

> 6b. Local Network Multiplayer

Some of our applications offer a local multiplayer mode in which devices connect directly to each other over the same Wi-Fi network or hotspot (local room discovery and a direct connection between devices). In this mode, your chosen nickname and in-game results are visible to the other players in the same room. This communication stays entirely within your local network — it does not pass through the internet and is not sent to us or to any external server.

> 6c. Notifications

Some of our applications can show optional local notifications (for example, reminders about in-game events). These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device — no push server is involved and no data is collected or transmitted for this purpose. Notifications are off by default or require your explicit permission (Android 13+), and can be disabled at any time in the application settings or in the system settings.

> 6d. In-App Purchases

Some of our applications offer optional in-app purchases of virtual items (for example, in-game currency or hints). All payments are processed by Google Play's billing system. We do not receive, process, or store your payment details (such as card numbers). Virtual items are for use within the application only, have no real-world monetary value, and are non-transferable. Refunds are handled in accordance with Google Play's refund policy.

> 7. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

> 8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or delete your data (if collected)
  • Withdraw consent to data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.
